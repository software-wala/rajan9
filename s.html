About Rajan

Rajan is a popular given name of Sanskrit origin, meaning "king" or "ruler." It is commonly used in India, Nepal, and other South Asian countries, and carries connotations of leadership, strength, and nobility.

The name Rajan represents qualities of leadership, responsibility, and a commanding presence. Those named Rajan are often seen as natural leaders with a strong sense of justice.

In various cultures, Rajan is not just a name but a title of respect and authority. It has been used historically to denote rulers and persons of high standing in the community.
